I'm **Rafael Ortiz** — an AI practitioner and software engineer focused on building systems where intelligence meets the real world. I work at the intersection of LLMs, IoT, and creative technology.

My projects range from platforms that wire AI agents into physical hardware to tools that use language models to amplify human creativity.

## What I Do

- **AI Agents & LLMs** — designing agentic systems powered by large language models, with a focus on real-world interaction and autonomy
- **IoT & Edge AI** — connecting AI reasoning to physical sensors and actuators so intelligent decisions can happen close to the hardware
- **Creative AI** — building tools that use language models to help people write, create, and express themselves in new ways
- **Open Source** — contributing to and maintaining tools that make AI more accessible

## Featured Projects

**[Clariot](https://github.com/dirakx1/clariot)** — an AI agent-based IoT development platform. Containerized LLM-powered agents sit between sensors and actuators, processing real-world data and producing real-world actions through a Sensor → Agent (LLM) → Actuator architecture.

**[PoemWeaver](https://github.com/dirakx1/poemweaver)** — a creative AI system that generates poems, stories, and haikus in your voice or in the style of famous authors, trained on your own writing.

## Clariot

**[github.com/dirakx1/clariot](https://github.com/dirakx1/clariot)**

An AI agent-based IoT development platform that enables containerized LLM-powered agents to interact with sensors and actuators across all layers of an IoT stack.

The platform follows a **Sensor → Agent (LLM) → Actuator** architecture: AI agents receive sensor data, reason over it using large language models, and produce real-world actions through actuators. Each agent runs in its own container, making the system modular, scalable, and deployable across heterogeneous hardware.

This brings the flexibility of LLM reasoning directly into physical systems — enabling adaptive, context-aware automation that goes beyond rigid rule-based IoT logic.

**Stack:** Python · LLMs · Docker · IoT protocols

## PoemWeaver

**[github.com/dirakx1/poemweaver](https://github.com/dirakx1/poemweaver)**

A creative AI system that generates poems, stories, and haikus using your own writing as the source of inspiration. PoemWeaver learns from your personal poems to produce new work that matches your voice and style — or channels the style of famous authors.

Whether you want to write like Neruda, Dickinson, or like yourself, PoemWeaver uses AI to extend your creative range without replacing your voice.

**Stack:** Python · Language Models · Creative AI
